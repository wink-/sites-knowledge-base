## The Cat Colony

Rob trapped, neutered/spayed, and rehomed 17 cats over ~3 years. One had a broken vertebrae and didn't make it -- all others placed in homes. A woman in Elmira who specializes in tough street cats helped socialize the wildest ones. Rob calls her a saint.

Cost: nearly $30K over 3 years, including nearly $7K for his best friend cat that passed from cancer. All while being a new homeowner, dealing with dad's sepsis, and Uber driving at night.
